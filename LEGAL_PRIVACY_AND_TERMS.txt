1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information you provide

- Address and location details used for bookings and service delivery
- Booking details: selected services, schedules, add-ons, notes, and related persons
- Payment-related information needed to process bookings and wallet activity
  (card/payment data is processed by our payment provider; we do not store full
  card numbers on our servers)

- Referral codes and referral-related activity

1.2 Information collected automatically
- Device information (device type, OS, app version)
- Log and usage data (screens viewed, actions in the app, crash/diagnostic data)

  nearby services and improve booking accuracy
- Push notification tokens (for example FCM) to send service alerts
- Chat/messaging content between you and service providers within the app

1.3 Permissions the app may request
Depending on your device and features you use, Squedio may ask for:
- Location (to personalize services and improve accuracy)
- Camera and photo library (profile photos and uploads)
- Microphone and speech recognition (voice input features)
- Contacts (optional social/connect features)
- Biometrics / Face ID (optional quick and secure authentication)
- Notifications (booking updates and alerts)

You can change these permissions in your device settings. Some features may not
work without certain permissions.


2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Create and manage your account
- Process bookings, payments, refunds, cancellations, and wallet settlements
- Connect you with service providers and enable in-app chat
- Send transactional emails, SMS/OTP, and push notifications
- Personalize content, recommendations, and service discovery
- Provide customer support and resolve disputes
- Improve safety, security, fraud prevention, and app performance
- Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service
- Operate referral and promotional programs


3. How We Share Information

We may share information with:
- Service providers you book with (as needed to fulfill the booking)
- Payment processors (for example Stripe) to handle payments securely

  process data on our behalf under contractual obligations
- Professional advisors or authorities when required by law or to protect rights,
  safety, and security

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share aggregated or de-identified information that cannot reasonably
identify you.


4. Payments

Payments are processed by third-party payment providers. Those providers process
payment data under their own privacy policies. Squedio receives payment status
and related transaction records needed to manage bookings and your wallet.


5. Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as your account is active and as needed to

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to

and we cannot guarantee absolute security.


7. Your Rights and Choices

Subject to applicable law, you may:

- Withdraw device permissions
- Request deletion of your account/data by contacting support@ondemand.com
- Request a copy of personal data we hold about you

We may need to verify your identity before responding. Some data may be retained
where required by law or for legitimate business purposes (for example completed
transactions).


8. Children’s Privacy

Squedio is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in
your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
If you believe a child has provided us data, contact support@ondemand.com and
we will take appropriate steps to delete it.


9. International Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than where you live. Where

10. Third-Party Links and Services

The Service may contain links to third-party websites or services. Their privacy

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated
